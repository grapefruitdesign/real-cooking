FAQ'S

IS THE FOOD ALLERGEN FREE?

No. We do not make our food in a facility dedicated to allergen-free packaging.

WHERE CAN I BUY THE BAKING KITS?

You can purchase The Ultimate Baking Set, The Deluxe Cookie Baking Set, The Chocolatey Cake Pops and Cones Set, and The Sprinkled Mini Donuts Set from most major retailers both online and in store. Some vendors are Wal-Mart, Toys R Us, Target, and Amazon.

WHERE CAN I BUY REFILL KITS?

You can purchase refill kits from most major retailers both online and in store. Some vendors are Wal-Mart, Toys R Us, Target, and Amazon.

HOW MUCH SUPERVISION IS REQUIRED?

We designed the Real Cooking line of toys to empower kids to cook for themselves. However, they will need help from an adult to turn on the oven, and take things in and out of the oven. Children should never be left unsupervised.

DO I HAVE TO USE THE KITS TO MAKE THE FOOD IN THE REFILL KIT?

It is not necessary to use the baking sets to make the food in the refill kits but it is helpful as the Real Cooking line is designed very specifically in regards to measurement, size and quantity. It will be helpful to have baking molds and measuring cups at the proper size.

HOW DO I STORE MY REFILL SET?

Please store all food mixes and decorations in a cool, dry place free from pests or molds. Baking tools can be stored the same way you store all your important kitchen tools.

HOW LONG ARE THE MIXES GOOD FOR BEFORE BAKING?

All food in the Real Cooking product line has a shelf life of two years when stored properly in a cool dry place before cooking.

MY FAMILY IS VEGAN OR HAS ALLERGIES. CAN WE SUBSTITUTE INGREDIENTS?

We cannot attest to cooking times, ratios, or final product of food when deviating from the instructions but we encourage experimentation in the kitchen and hope you will share your results with us! Message us on Facebook to get in touch! https://www.facebook.com/RealCookingProducts
